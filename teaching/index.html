About Me

I am an assistant professor at the university of Bergamo (UNIBG). My research interests include particle phenomenology, precision measurements, and new physics searches.

Before joining UNIBG, I obtained my PhD from Imperial College London, held a postdoctoral position at the Swiss Federal Institute of Technology in Lausanne and I was a senior research fellow at CERN in Geneva.
