About

I am a tenure-track Assistant Professor of Physics at the University of Bergamo. My research focuses on searching for new physics at the LHC with the LHCb experiment, with particular emphasis on long-lived particles and electroweak processes. I am passionate about open science, reproducible analyses, and mentoring the next generation of physicists.
